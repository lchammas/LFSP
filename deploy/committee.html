Chair: Joseph Gafton

Hi! I’m Chair of LFSP and am responsible for overseeing the organisation’s activities as well as guiding policy groups and getting involved in workshops and events.

I joined Imperial this year as a ‘Direct Entry’ student to the third year of the Medical course having finished my BA at Cambridge. I studied Preclinical Medicine for the first two years and History in third year focussing on Modern Political Thought, the African American Civil Rights Movement and Subsaharan Africa.

I’m particularly interested in the politics of healthcare systems, science’s impact on gender identities and the tension between democracy and expert-led decision making. Distressed by most strenuous exercise, I play tennis in the summer and my spare time is filled mainly by musical endeavours on the piano, guitar + harmonica.
